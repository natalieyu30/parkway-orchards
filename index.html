About Us

Growing Farm Food & Activities Since 1970

At FAIRVIEW VALLEY ORCHARDS, you get fresh, local produce grown right here in Niagara-On-The-Lake. And we don't just grow it—we use the ingredients in our bakery and café to make the most delicious farm food you've ever tasted. Everything we grow is available to you right here on the farm. We want your whole family to enjoy their time here.

During the picking season we offer pick your own cherries, peaches, plums, nectarines, apples and grapes. Bite into one of these freshly picked fruits and the amazing sweetness and juiciness will not soon leave your memories. Stop by for a visit to pick up some fresh fruits from our very own farm or some of our fantastic home baked pies.

FAQ

When are you open?

We are open EVERYDAY, including holidays, from June 15th to October 31st . See our daily hours and directions to our farm here . Keep in mind that there will be days when the farm is closed or closing early depending on the weather or the number of visitors. Please check the picking page frequently for the status of the farm before planning any trips.

Can I bring my dog?

We know that your furry friends are part of the family and they are welcome in the outdoor areas of our farm, as long as they are friendly and on a leash. In accordance with health regulations, no animals (except service dogs) are permitted in the market or on the patio. We have some picnic tables away from the main Café Patio, where guests are welcome to sit with their pets.

What produce is in season now?

Please see our picking page for a list of what is being freshly picked. We update this list every single day!

Are you GMO free?

Yes, all the produce grown on our farm is GMO free. Generally, only large-scale crops have GMO seed options. GMO seeds are not practical for small scale farmers like us. There is so much false information about GMOs. Whenever you can, visit the farm for yourself and talk to the farmer. When you do this, you are making an educated decision about how you will spend your food dollars, and this makes a huge difference to protect our food system and our planet.

How ripe are our peaches?

Because we sell directly off the farm, we have the advantage to let our peaches ripen on the trees for as long as possible. Peaches that are grown for shipping cross-country have to be picked early while they are still very green. That being said, we don’t want to let our peaches ripen to the point that they are soft while they are still on the trees. We sell them at the “firm-ripe” stage, so you can get them home and spread them out gently for the last few days where they can ripen in peace. From the time of purchase, expect 2-3 days before they are ready to eat, and 5-7 days before they are fully ripened and ready to can.
